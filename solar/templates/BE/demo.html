About Us

The solar industry, like most sectors, is rapidly embracing ways to analyze and crunch data to solve various problems. Setting up a solar plant in a way as to produce maximum output is a challenging and time consuming task. Before the installation of a solar power plant, various factors like optimal location, areas in shadow and total space available to install PV modules, have to be considered and on the basis of this, the solar power plant is to be installed. After the installation of solar modules, self learning algorithms need to be deployed to build a more accurate forecasting and analyzing model. Through our project, we intend to ease out the process of installing the solar power plant and also its operation and maintenance after installation.
